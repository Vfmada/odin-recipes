Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro is a beloved Brazilian confection celebrated for its rich chocolate taste and velvety texture. Crafted by cooking sweetened condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter until it thickens, each bite-sized treat is then rolled in chocolate sprinkles for a satisfying crunch.

While traditionally served at birthday parties and special occasions, brigadeiros can also be enjoyed as a quick indulgence any time. Some variations introduce flavors like coconut, hazelnut, or even a touch of coffee, ensuring there's always room for creativity. Whether you stick to the classic recipe or experiment with twists, these little chocolate truffles never fail to delight.

Ingredients

  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Unsweetened cocoa
  • Butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. Put a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add a can of condensed milk, 3 tablespoons of cocoa and a tablespoon of butter.
  3. Cook and stir until thickened.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a shallow bowl and let it cool.
  5. Pinch off some of the dough and roll into a small ball; place on a plate.
  6. Repeat with the remaining mixture.
  7. Roll each brigadeiro in chocolate sprinkles.
  8. Enjoy it like that or put it in the fridge until cold.
